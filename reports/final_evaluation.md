## Key Findings

1. **DPO alignment consistently outperforms both prior stages** — particularly on safety-critical questions (heart attack, stroke) where it adds mechanism explanations and named treatments.
2. **The SFT model was a major leap from baseline** — moving from harmful omissions to accurate, actionable responses. DPO was a refinement, not a transformation.
3. **DPO improved tone and structure** — responses feel warmer, more organized, and more motivating without sacrificing accuracy.
4. **Clinical nuance increased with DPO** — specific named treatments (tPA, Tamiflu, CBT-I), time windows (4.5 hours for stroke), and mechanistic explanations (why to chew aspirin) only appear in DPO outputs.
5. **Safety improvement is the most critical gain** — for a healthcare assistant, incorrect emergency guidance can cause harm. The DPO model is the safest of the three.

---

## Final Verdict

The three-stage pipeline (non-instruction FT → SFT → DPO) produced progressively better results. The DPO-aligned model is clearly the best for deployment as a Healthcare FAQ Assistant, offering accurate, safe, professional, and nuanced responses across all tested questions.
